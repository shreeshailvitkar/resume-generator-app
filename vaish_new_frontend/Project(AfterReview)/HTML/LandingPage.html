How To Make A Resume

1.Open Canvas
Open up Canva and search for "Resume" to start designing your own.

2.Find The Right Template
Choose from designed resume templates. Take your pick from styles like modern or simple layouts.

3.Personalize your resume
After picking a template, Edit your Profile in a few clicks. Upload your own photo and you can edit the profile as much as you want.

4.Download your Resume
Download high-quality prints of your resumes through Canvas. Or, save your design as a PDF file.
